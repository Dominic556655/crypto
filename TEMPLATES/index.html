What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is an innovative payment network and a new kind of money...
Bitcoin is a type of digital money (cryptocurrency) that allows people to invest, send and receive payments over the internet without needing a bank or central authority.
It uses blockchain technology, a public digital ledger where all transactions are recorded and cannot be changed.
Bitcoin offers financial freedom and inclusion to people worldwide, including those without access to banks. It’s an investment opportunity, a hedge against inflation, and a way to diversify your assets in a fast-changing digital world.

Why is Bitcoin Valuable?
  1. Decentralized & Transparent
    Unlike traditional money, Bitcoin is not controlled by any single entity. It runs on a public network (blockchain) that anyone can verify, making it transparent and resistant to fraud.
  2. Limited Supply
    Only 21 million bitcoins will ever exist. This limited supply makes it similar to gold – as demand increases, its value tends to rise over time.
  3. Borderless Transactions
    With Bitcoin, you can send money across countries in minutes, without high fees or delays often experienced in bank transfers.
  4. Secure & Private
    Transactions use strong cryptography to ensure security and privacy. Only you control your wallet.
  5. Growing Acceptance
    Today, thousands of companies and millions of individuals accept Bitcoin, and it continues to gain popularity as a store of value and means of payment.

⚠️ Important to know:
  • It's price can change quickly (highly volatile).
  • Transactions are peer-to-peer, meaning they go directly from sender to receiver.
  • It is not controlled by any government or bank, making it decentralized.
